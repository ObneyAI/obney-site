Reason Stack

The engineering core under the promise.

Reason Stack is the name for the methodology and technical spine behind the factory: durable state, managed workflows, native agent interfaces, and operator surfaces built for humans and agents together.

State substrate Event-sourced systems that keep every change inspectable.
Workflow runtime Delegatable behavior trees, approvals, traces, and retries.
Operator surface Interfaces where people steer, inspect, and accept the work.
Transmission The capability moves into your team instead of staying rented.

No black box.

No hour theater.

The commercial unit is shipped capability, not bodies on a timer.

No throwaway pilots.

Radar
